Terms of Service & Privacy Policy
1. Introduction

Welcome to Kisan Connect. By registering as either a Farmer or a Buyer, you agree to abide by the rules of this marketplace. Our goal is to facilitate direct, fair, and transparent trade.

2. User Responsibilities

Farmers: You agree to provide accurate information regarding the type, quantity, and quality of your harvest. Misrepresentation of crop yield or use of unverified chemicals may result in platform suspension.

Buyers: You agree to negotiate in good faith and honor accepted price agreements. Payment must be initiated securely through the platform upon logistics confirmation.

3. Transactions & Logistics

Kisan Connect acts solely as a technological facilitator. While we provide tools for price negotiation and logistics tracking, the final contract of sale is strictly between the Farmer and the Buyer. Kisan Connect is not liable for spoiled goods during third-party transit.

4. Data Privacy

Your personal data, including farm locations and business addresses, are stored securely. We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketing agencies. Transactional data may be used anonymously to provide market price trends to the community.

*Note: This is a prototype application built for demonstration purposes. No real monetary transactions will occur.
