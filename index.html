Terms of Service & Privacy Policy
1. Introduction

Welcome to FarmLink. By registering as either a Farmer or a Buyer, you agree to abide by the rules of this marketplace. Our goal is to facilitate direct, fair, and transparent trade.

2. User Responsibilities

Farmers: You agree to provide accurate information regarding the type, quantity, and quality of your harvest.

Buyers: You agree to negotiate in good faith and honor accepted price agreements.

3. Transactions & Logistics

FarmLink acts solely as a technological facilitator. While we provide tools for price negotiation and logistics tracking, the final contract of sale is strictly between the Farmer and the Buyer.

*Note: This is a prototype application built for demonstration purposes. No real monetary transactions will occur.
